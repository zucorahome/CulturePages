About Us

We are Canada's Smarter Living Company. Our innovative repair and replacement programs for furniture, appliances, and home systems protect your budget from the unexpected, and our Smarter Home Products give you confidence that you're positively impacting your family's well-being and your environment.


Helping create a world of clean homes, healthy families, and intelligent lifestyles is rooted in our corporate operating standards. This extends to how we source our products, our corporate culture, and our dedication to our Members, plan holders, and industry partners.


Located in London, Ontario, we're proud to be recognized as one of the best places to work, and we're passionate about delivering on our values and commitments.

Our Commitments

Sustainability

Our corporate social responsibility is deeply rooted in our mission to provide products, services, and operating standards that incorporate recycled materials and sustainable processes. Our ZU Collection products are eco-friendly, non-toxic, cruelty free, and made in Canada.

Because we believe in building a better tomorrow, our Environmental Committee actively implements greener procedures, and inspires all Zucorians to look for ways to reduce our carbon footprint. Some of our initiatives include reducing waste and energy consumption, improved recycling programs, and using plants to naturally improve air quality.

As part of our commitment to sustainable and ethical business practices, we’re initiating a procurement policy that defines how we do business. This includes responsible acquisition of goods and services, transparent processes and procedures, and aligning ourselves with like-minded vendors.

Community

Investing in our community is an integral part of our commitment to support the communities where we live, work and play.

Because we believe that business isn't just about numbers—it’s really about people, our Culture and Community Committee actively seeks ways to make a positive impact in our communities. We invest our time and resources by partnering with local and national organizations that support youth education, health and well—being, and the environment.

At our home base, community is at the heart of our corporate culture, and collaboration is in our DNA. The ZucoraHome family extends far beyond our team to include our customers and our service and retail partners. We believe in building relationships, and we’re in it for the long haul, together.

Innovation

Our successful track record of delivering service, repair, and replacement programs is marked by a 40-year history of understanding the needs of our customers, anticipating change, and transforming home management with modern solutions. By combining our people, professional services and technology, we deliver smarter living solutions designed for Canadians who are seeking a convenient and eco-friendly lifestyle.

Because we believe in building leaders of teams, and teams of leaders, we foster a culture of creativity, trust, and participation. Through our Product Advisory Committee, Zucorians ensure that we’re meeting and anticipating the needs of our customers by testing our products and reviewing market trends. This approach empowers our teams and drives our forward thinking processes and procedures.
